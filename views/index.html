About

Hello World!

My name is Lawrence Liu from San Francisco, CA. I was born in California but lived in Taiwan up until college. I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2016, a year earlier than my expected graduation. While I studied Economics and worked in investment banks, I am entrepreneurial at heart and creative by nature. I was Co-Founder of two e-commerce startups, Morph Auto Design and Bovi Flowers. At both businesses, I directed marketing strategy and product development. However, I soon realized that in today’s business environment, there were missing skills that I needed to more efficiently engage with the market. I recall my business professor in Berkeley saying, “Learn to program or be programmed,” so I decided to join UC Berkeley’s Coding Program. After 3 months of intensive work, I am now a Full Stack Developer capable of building MERN stack applications. I am always eager to take on new projects and make new friends. So if you have job proposals or just simply want to connect, feel free to reach out via the contact page.

Cheers,
Lawrence L.
