Overview

Fashion Scraper is a Node.js application that allows users to scrape articles from Hypebeast.com (street fashion blog) using Cheerio. The app also allows users to view, comment, save, and delete articles of their choice. The front-end display of the app uses Handlebars and Bootstrap.
