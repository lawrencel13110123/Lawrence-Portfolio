Overview

RouteMe is a Javascript application that creates a clear cost and time comparison between driving and taking public transport for a given trip.

We recognize that global warming is a real issue and wiser decisions between driving and public transport can reduce pollution. Besides, gas money can slowly eat up your wallet. People drive everyday but seldom do they pay close attention to how much they are spending on gas. Finally, traffic sucks. Bad traffic is a pain for everyone and the right mode of transit can be the solution. RouteMe is the comprehensive answer to all of these problems
